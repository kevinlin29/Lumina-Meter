Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

Lumina is a light meter app that processes camera data entirely on your device. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

Data Collection

Lumina does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

Camera Access

Lumina requires camera access to function as a light meter. Camera frames are processed in real-time on your device using Metal GPU acceleration and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Lumina does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Storage

Your settings (ISO, aperture, metering mode, film stock selection) are stored locally on your device. No data leaves your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via GitHub Issues.
