LiDAR Distance

Know exactly how far your subject is

On Pro iPhones, Lumina uses the built-in LiDAR scanner to measure real-time distance to your subject. See precise depth readings right in the viewfinder — invaluable for zone focusing, hyperfocal calculations, and nailing critical focus on manual lenses.
